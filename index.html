About myself

I am a computational mathematics/science researcher at Oden Institute, supervised by Prof. J. Tinsley Oden and Prof. Omar Ghattas. I am interested in predictive modeling, Bayesian statistics, and optimization for systems governed by partial differential equations. My dissertation work focuses on algorithm design, predictive modeling, and uncertainty quantification for material self-assembly.
